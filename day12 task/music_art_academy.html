About Us

Where Passion Meets Purpose

Founded in 2004, Melodia Academy has been Hyderabad's most trusted music and arts institution for two decades. We blend classical traditions with modern creativity.

Our faculty includes national award winners, international performers, and dedicated educators who bring the best out of every student — from toddlers to adults.
