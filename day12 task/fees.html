Financial Support

Scholarships & Concessions

We believe no talented student should be held back by finances. Melodia offers three types of scholarships reviewed each academic year.

Merit Scholarship

Up to 50% fee waiver for students who demonstrate exceptional aptitude in their audition or entrance assessment.

Need-based Aid

Up to 30% fee reduction for economically weaker sections, subject to documentation and counsellor review.

Sibling Discount

10% discount on total fees when two or more siblings enroll simultaneously in any program.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our fees and enrollment policies.

Is there a registration fee?

Yes, a one-time registration fee of ₹500 applies when you first enroll. This covers your student ID, portal access setup, and welcome kit. It is non-refundable.

Can I change my program after enrolling?

Yes! You can switch programs at the end of any month. Simply inform your counsellor 7 days in advance and the new fee structure will apply from the next billing cycle.

What is the refund policy?

If you wish to discontinue, fees for completed sessions are non-refundable. Fees for remaining sessions in the paid month can be refunded within 7 working days of application.

Are there any discounts for annual payment?

Yes! Pay for 10 months and get 2 months free (an effective 16.7% discount). Quarterly payments receive a 5% discount. Contact our office for the annual payment invoice.

Is the free trial class really free?

Absolutely — no strings attached. Your first class is completely free and there is no obligation to enroll. We simply want you to experience Melodia firsthand before deciding.
