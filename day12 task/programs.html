Curriculum

Structured Learning Path

Our curriculum follows a proven progression model adapted from Trinity College London and Indian classical systems.

Foundation

Beginner Level

  • Introduction to instrument / art form
  • Basic notation & music theory
  • Fundamental techniques & posture
  • Simple songs & exercises
  • Group participation activities
Development

Intermediate Level

  • Scales, arpeggios & advanced theory
  • Classical repertoire & ragas
  • Performance technique & stage craft
  • Exam preparation (Trinity / ABRSM)
  • First solo recital opportunity
Mastery

Advanced Level

  • Concert-level repertoire
  • Improvisation & composition
  • Music psychology & practice methods
  • Mentorship & teaching practicum
  • Annual showcase performance
