About Melodia

Two decades of nurturing artists, shaping futures, and building a creative community in Hyderabad.

Our Mission

Where Every Note Tells a Story

Founded in 2004 by Dr. Priya Nair and a group of passionate artists, Melodia Academy was born with a simple yet powerful vision — to make world-class arts education accessible to every child and adult in Hyderabad.

Over two decades, we've grown from a small studio in Banjara Hills into Telangana's most respected music and arts institution, nurturing over 2,400 students across 12 disciplines. Our approach combines the rigor of classical training with the freedom of modern creative expression.

Whether you're a 4-year-old beginning your first piano lesson or a working professional discovering a new passion at 50, Melodia is your home.

Our Values

The Principles That Guide Us

Everything we do is rooted in these core beliefs about music, art, and education.

Excellence

We hold ourselves and our students to the highest standards — not for perfection, but for consistent growth and artistic integrity in everything we do.

Nurturing Growth

Every student starts somewhere. We celebrate progress at every stage, cultivating confidence alongside technical skill in a supportive, encouraging environment.

Community

Music and art are meant to be shared. We foster a vibrant community where students, families, and faculty grow together through concerts, exhibitions, and collaborative projects.

Cultural Heritage

We honour India's rich artistic traditions — Carnatic, Hindustani, Bharatanatyam — while also embracing global influences and contemporary forms.

Innovation

From online learning platforms to digital arts integration, we constantly evolve our teaching methods to stay relevant and effective for today's learners.

Inclusivity

Arts education should be for everyone. We offer merit scholarships, flexible fee structures, and weekend batches to remove barriers to creative learning.

Our Journey

Two Decades of Excellence

Key milestones that shaped Melodia into what it is today.

04
2004

Melodia Founded

Dr. Priya Nair opens a small studio in Banjara Hills with 3 faculty members and 40 students. Piano, vocals, and guitar offered.

08
2008

First Annual Concert

100+ students perform at Ravindra Bharathi in Melodia's debut annual recital, receiving a standing ovation and media coverage.

12
2012

Dance & Fine Arts Added

Expansion into Bharatanatyam, Kuchipudi, oil painting, and sculpture brings the program count to 9 disciplines.

16
2016

Best Academy Award

Telangana State government recognizes Melodia with the Best Cultural Institution of the Year award.

20
2020

Online Learning Launch

During the pandemic, Melodia pivots online — launching India's first fully integrated music & arts e-learning platform.

24
2024

20th Anniversary & 2000+ Students

Melodia celebrates 20 years with its largest annual concert featuring 300 performers, and crosses 2,000 active students.
