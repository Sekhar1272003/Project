Essential Tips

Before You Travel

Must-know information to prepare for your journey across India.

Visa & Documents

Most international visitors need a valid passport (6+ months validity) and an Indian visa. Apply online through e-Visa for quick processing. Keep copies of documents in separate locations.

Currency & Money

Indian Rupee (INR) is the official currency. ATMs are widely available in cities. Notify your bank before travel. Carry some cash for small vendors and remote areas.

Health & Vaccinations

Consult a travel doctor 4-6 weeks before. Common vaccinations: Typhoid, Hepatitis A, Japanese Encephalitis. Carry travel insurance and prescription medications in original packaging.

Communication

Get an Indian SIM card or activate international roaming. WiFi is available in hotels, cafes. Download offline maps of your destinations using Google Maps.

Food & Water Safety

Drink bottled or purified water. Eat cooked hot food. Avoid raw salads and street food if you have a sensitive stomach. Carry anti-diarrhea medication just in case.

Weather & Clothing

Pack light, breathable clothes for summer. Bring warm layers for winter and hill stations. Comfortable walking shoes are essential. Respect dress codes at religious sites.
