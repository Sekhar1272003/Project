Our Story
Why GreenTrail

The Difference Is In the Details

Every element of a GreenTrail journey is designed with intention — for you, for wildlife, and for the communities that call these wild places home.

Expert-Led Safety

All guides hold wilderness first aid certification, and each route is risk-assessed with real-time weather monitoring.

Carbon-Neutral Travel

Every trip is fully offset through verified reforestation and renewable energy projects. We track every kilo of CO₂.

Small Group Experience

Maximum 12 guests per expedition means personalised guiding, minimal ecosystem impact, and real connections.

Eco Lodges Only

We partner exclusively with solar-powered, rainwater-harvesting, community-owned eco-lodges and camps.

Photography Support

Dedicated photography hours at every stop, with pro tips from our in-house wildlife photographer guides.

Conservation Projects

Opt into hands-on volunteering — plant trees, count wildlife, or join coral nursery programmes on the ground.
