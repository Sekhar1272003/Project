Insider Knowledge

Essential Dining Tips

Meal Times

Indians typically lunch from 1–3pm and dinner from 8–10pm. Thalis (set meals) offer the best value at lunchtime — often ₹150–400 for unlimited refills.

Spice Levels

Always specify "medium spicy" when ordering. Indian "mild" can be fiery for international palates. North Indian food is generally less spicy than South Indian cuisine.

Vegetarian Heaven

India is paradise for vegetarians — nearly 40% of the population is vegetarian. Every restaurant will have extensive veg menus. Look for "Pure Veg" signs on restaurants.
