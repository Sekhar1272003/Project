Getting Around

Navigate India Like a Local

India has the world's 4th largest railway network plus domestic flights, auto-rickshaws & scenic road trips to connect you across the subcontinent.

Transport Options

How to Get Around

Indian Railways

The world's largest employer — 67,000 km of track connecting every corner of India. Book on IRCTC for best prices and seat reservations.

BookingUp to 120 days ahead
Sleeper₹300–₹800
AC 3-tier₹800–₹2,500

State & Sleeper Buses

State-run KSRTC, MSRTC and private Volvo buses connect cities and towns affordably. Overnight buses are great for saving on accommodation costs.

Hours24/7 on key routes
AC Sleeper₹500–₹1,800
Ordinary₹100–₹400

Auto-Rickshaw

The iconic yellow-and-black three-wheeler — the soul of Indian city travel. Always ask for meter or agree on a price before boarding.

Hours5am–11pm
Base Fare₹25
Per km₹10–₹15

Metro Rail

Delhi, Mumbai, Bengaluru, Chennai, Hyderabad and 18 other cities have modern metro systems. Fast, air-conditioned and affordable for city travel.

Hours6am – 11pm
Ticket₹10–₹60
AvailableEvery 3–5 min

Domestic Flights

IndiGo, Air India & SpiceJet connect 100+ airports across India. For journeys over 1,000km, flying can be faster and sometimes cheaper than train.

Book Ahead2–8 weeks for best price
BudgetFrom ₹2,500
Routes700+ daily

Ola & Uber Cabs

App-based cabs are widely available in all major cities. Clean, air-conditioned and transparent pricing — ideal for families or those with luggage.

Hours24/7
Base Fare₹200 /8km
Airport₹400–₹900 typical

Popular Routes

Recommended Travel Routes

Delhi → Agra (Taj Mahal)

Take the Gatimaan Express — India's fastest train — to Agra in just 1h 40min. Or opt for the Shatabdi Express for a comfortable morning journey.

1h 40min ₹755 Gatimaan Express

Mumbai → Goa

The famous Konkan Railway ride along the Western Ghats and coast — one of India's most scenic train journeys, passing waterfalls and sea views.

8–12 hours ₹400–₹2,000 Konkan Railway

Bengaluru → Mysuru

A short train or drive through Karnataka's silk country — Mysuru's grand Maharaja's Palace and sandalwood markets make it an unmissable day trip.

2 hours ₹120–₹300 Shatabdi Express

Delhi → Manali Road Trip

The classic Himalayan road adventure — through the Kullu Valley into mountain country. Best done in June–September. Take a Volvo overnight bus from Delhi.

14–16 hours ₹1,200–₹1,800 Volvo Bus
